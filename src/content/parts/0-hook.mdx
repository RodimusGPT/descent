## You typed something and hit enter

A fraction of a second later, words appeared. It feels like the machine *understood*
you. What actually happened is stranger and more mechanical: the model read your text,
turned it into numbers, and predicted the single most plausible **next token** — then
did it again, and again, until it decided to stop.

That is the whole trick. Everything below this point — the transformer, the weights, the
serving stack, the silicon — exists to answer one question fast: *given everything so
far, what token comes next?*

Follow the token down. This is a descent.
